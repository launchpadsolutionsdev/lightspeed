Features

Work at Lightspeed.

Four purpose-built tools that tackle the most time-consuming tasks in charitable gaming and nonprofit operations. Your time is valuable — stop spending it on work AI can do better and faster.

✍️

Draft Assistant

Generate on-brand social media posts, emails, press releases, and newsletters in seconds. Lightspeed knows your organization's voice because it's been trained on your actual content — so you can write faster.

  • Social media posts & captions
  • Email campaigns & newsletters
  • Press releases & media advisories
  • Trained on your brand voice & past campaigns
📧

Response Assistant

Turn customer inquiries into professional, empathetic responses instantly. Built on a knowledge base specific to your organization, Response Assistant handles winner questions, support tickets, and donor communications with consistency and care.

  • AI-powered reply generation
  • Dedicated knowledge base (FAQ, draw schedules, AGCO rules)
  • Consistent, on-brand tone across every reply
  • Response history tracking
📊

Insights Engine

Upload your reports and instantly generate beautiful dashboards with charts, metrics, and actionable insights. Designed for seamless integration with BUMP Raffle and other charitable gaming service providers — just export your data and let Lightspeed do the rest. No spreadsheet skills required.

  • Seamless BUMP Raffle and other provider data integration
  • Automated chart & dashboard generation
  • Key metrics at a glance
  • Export-ready visualizations for reports & presentations
🔄

List Normalizer

Transform messy customer data into clean, formatted lists ready for Mailchimp and your email platform. What used to take hours now takes 30 seconds — trained on real-world data normalization tasks from active lottery operations.

  • Auto-format names & emails
  • Remove invalid entries & deduplicate records
  • One-click Mailchimp export
  • Trained on thousands of real data cleanup tasks
