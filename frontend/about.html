About Us

Built by operators,
for operators

Lightspeed was born on the front lines of charitable lottery operations — where small teams do big things with limited time and resources.

Our Mission

Give every nonprofit team the tools that used to be reserved for enterprises

Charitable lottery operators and nonprofit teams deserve the same AI-powered efficiency that Fortune 500 companies take for granted. Lightspeed exists to close that gap — delivering purpose-built tools that handle the repetitive, time-consuming work so your team can focus on what actually moves the needle: fundraising, community impact, and growth.

Our Vision

A world where team size doesn’t limit what you can accomplish

We’re building toward a future where a three-person nonprofit team can operate with the speed, consistency, and reach of an organization ten times its size. Not by working harder, but by having AI tools that truly understand their work — tools that learn, adapt, and get smarter with every interaction.

About Our Founder

Torin Gunnell

Founder & Creator of Lightspeed

As Director of Lotteries at the Thunder Bay Regional Health Sciences Foundation, Torin runs one of Canada’s largest hospital 50/50 lottery programs — a program that has generated over $180 million in total revenue for patient care.

But running a lottery of that scale with a small team meant relentless pressure on every front: thousands of customer inquiries per draw cycle, constant marketing demands, raw data that needed hours of manual processing, and contact lists that had to be cleaned and formatted before every campaign. The workload was unsustainable — and the off-the-shelf AI tools available simply didn’t understand the nuances of lottery operations.

So Torin built Lightspeed. Starting from real-world pain points, he designed a platform trained on tens of thousands of actual customer interactions, marketing campaigns, and operational scenarios. The result is an AI toolkit that doesn’t just generate generic responses — it genuinely understands the work, learns your organization’s voice, and gets smarter every time you use it.

What started as an internal solution for one lottery team has grown into a platform serving charitable gaming operators who face the same challenge: doing more with less, without sacrificing quality or compliance.

Our Principles

What we believe

Privacy First

Your data is never used to train AI models. Every organization’s knowledge base is isolated, encrypted, and fully under their control.

Built for Small Teams

Every feature is designed for teams of 1–10 people who wear multiple hats. No enterprise bloat, no features you’ll never use.

Radically Simple

If it needs a training manual, we haven’t finished building it. Lightspeed should feel intuitive from the first click — no prompt engineering required.

Gets Smarter Over Time

Every correction, every piece of feedback, every knowledge base entry makes Lightspeed more accurate. It compounds intelligence instead of losing it.
